Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book via WhatsApp (fastest), call/text, email, or use the contact form above. I'll confirm your appointment and discuss details like location, timing, and any specific requirements.

Do I need to provide my own hair?

Yes, typically clients provide their own hair/extensions. I can recommend brands and quantities based on your chosen style. If you need help sourcing hair, let me know during booking.

What's your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to a rescheduled appointment within 30 days.

How long do appointments usually take?

It varies by style! Small box braids can take 8-10 hours, while simple cornrows might take 2-3 hours. Check the pricing page for estimated durations, and I'll give you a more accurate timeframe when you book.

Do you charge a travel fee?

I serve the Greater Metro Area (30-mile radius) with no travel fee. Locations outside this area may incur a small travel charge, which we'll discuss during booking.

What forms of payment do you accept?

I accept cash or Mpesa. A deposit is required when booking, with the balance due upon completion of service.

Can you work with kids?

Absolutely! I love working with children and have special patience and techniques to make the experience enjoyable for them. Kids' styles are typically simpler and quicker.

What should I prepare before you arrive?

Please have your hair clean and detangled. Set up a comfortable space with good lighting, a chair, mirror, and access to an outlet. Have your hair/extensions ready, and any hair accessories you'd like to use.
