Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I book an appointment?

Via WhatsApp (fastest), call/text, email, or the form above. I'll confirm your appointment and discuss location, timing, and requirements.

Do I need to provide my own hair?

Yes, clients typically provide their own hair/extensions. I can recommend brands and quantities based on your chosen style — just ask during booking.

What's your cancellation policy?

Please give at least 24 hours notice. Deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to a rescheduled appointment within 30 days.

How long do appointments take?

It varies! Small box braids take 8–10 hours, simple cornrows 2–3 hours. Check the pricing page for estimates — I'll give you an accurate timeframe when you book.

Do you charge a travel fee?

Travel within the main service areas (Nairobi, Thika, Machakos, Limuru) is generally included. Locations further out may incur a small charge — discussed at booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash or M-Pesa. A deposit is required to secure your slot, with the balance due on completion of service.

Do you style children's hair?

Absolutely! I love working with kids and use gentle techniques to make the experience fun and comfortable. Kids' styles are typically quicker too.

How should I prepare before you arrive?

Have your hair clean and detangled. Set up a comfortable space with good lighting, a chair, mirror, and access to a power outlet. Have your hair/extensions ready to go.
