Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I book an appointment?

Via WhatsApp (fastest), call, email, or the form above. I will confirm your appointment and discuss location, timing, and requirements.

Do I need to provide my own hair?

Yes, clients provide their own hair and extensions. I can recommend brands and quantities for your chosen style — just ask during booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please give at least 24 hours notice. Deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to a rescheduled appointment within 30 days.

How long do appointments take?

It varies. Small box braids take 8 to 10 hours, simple cornrows 2 to 3 hours. Check the pricing page for estimates per style.

Do you charge a travel fee?

Travel within the main service areas is generally included. Locations further out may incur a small charge, discussed at booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash or M-Pesa. A deposit is required to secure your slot, with the balance due on completion of service.
