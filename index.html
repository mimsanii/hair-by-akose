My Journey

How It All Started

“You don’t have to feel pain to be beautiful.”

Hair by Akose transforms your look without disrupting your comfort. We specialize in stunning hairstyles and cute hair accessories, delivered directly to you.

For the past 6 years, I’ve been transforming looks across Nairobi, Thika, Machakos, and Limuru. My journey began with a deep love for art expressed through hair — and a determination to do things differently.

What started as a passion has grown into a mobile beauty service that prioritises comfort, care, and confidence. Whether you’re at home, in the office, or at a hotel, I bring the full salon experience to you.
