My Journey

How It All Started

From Passion to Profession

Hair by Akose transforms your look without disrupting your comfort. We specialize in stunning hairstyles and cute hair accessories, delivered directly to you.

For the past 6 years, I’ve been transforming looks across Nairobi, Thika, Machakos, and Limuru. My journey began with a deep love for art expressed through hair—and a determination to do things differently after experiencing painful styling that made beauty feel like suffering. I believed then, and still believe now, that you don’t have to feel pain to be beautiful.

What started as a passion has grown into a mobile beauty service that prioritizes comfort, care, and confidence. Whether you’re at home, in the office, or at a hotel, I bring the full salon experience to you. Every client deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and pampered—without the stress of traveling to a salon. That’s what Hair by Akose does: bringing beauty to you, at your convenience.
